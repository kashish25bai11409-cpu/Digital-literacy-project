# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s
- Think before posting anything online.
- Use respectful and polite language in comments and messages.
- Protect your personal information and adjust privacy settings.
- Verify information before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Use social media for learning, networking, and positive communication.

## Don’ts
- Do not share fake news or unverified information.
- Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful content.
- Avoid sharing private information of others without their permission.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online harassment.
- Avoid posting content that could harm your reputation or future career.
