## Project Overview
This project focuses on developing awareness and understanding of digital literacy and responsible online behavior. In today's digital world, people interact with various online platforms for communication, collaboration, learning, and information sharing. The aim of this project is to promote safe, responsible, and effective use of digital technologies.

In this project, the role of a **Digital Ambassador** is highlighted. The tasks completed in this project focus on building a strong digital identity, understanding professional communication, exploring digital collaboration platforms, and spreading awareness about cyber safety. Through these activities, the project encourages students to become responsible digital citizens.

---

## Project Objectives
- To understand the concept of digital literacy.
- To learn how to build and maintain a professional digital identity.
- To explore digital tools and platforms used for collaboration.
- To practice proper email communication and etiquette.
- To spread awareness about cybercrime and online safety.
